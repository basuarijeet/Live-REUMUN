REVA University Model United Nations 2023

All About REUMUN

The REVA University Model United Nations is the largest platform you'll ever get to express your views on rapidly emerging global issues. With an Organizing Committee of over 50 members, excellent infrastructure and global presence, our position as one of Bengaluru's best MUN becomes undeniable.

We are delighted to announce our 1st Edition of Model United Nations, to give you a unique opportunity to represent yourself and be the voice of a million. Every edition aims to live up to our REUMUN's motto - "Offer equal opportunities to all, so that they rise to their fullest potential".

REUMUN 2023 will commence on the 7th of November, and will carry on over a span of 2 days. Will you be a samaritan and save countless lives, or will you overshadow the tyranny of Caligula as you hold your own country's interests above the world's?

We can't wait to see what you do.
